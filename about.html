My natural inclination is to make things that are cerebral, introspective, and infused with a sense of magic. Much of my work deals with human suffering, often pairing it with humor. I would like my work to offer the opportunity to transcend the limitations of the human experience, particularly to outgrow social conditioning and make ourselves better contributors to a larger community. This starts with first honoring ourselves, exactly as we are, with no judgment or attempts to make ourselves into what we think we should be.
